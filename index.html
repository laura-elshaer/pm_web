Meet Sarah: A Product Manager's Journey

Follow Sarah as she navigates real PM challenges. Click through her story to learn key concepts!

Sarah - Product Manager
"My team wants to build 10 new features, but we only have time for 2. How do I decide what matters most?"

The Challenge: Prioritization

Sarah needs to prioritize features based on impact and effort, not just what's loudest.

Sarah - Analyzing
"First, I need to understand the REAL user problems. Let me talk to users before jumping to solutions!"

✅ The Approach: Discovery First

Sarah conducts user interviews to understand pain points before deciding what to build.

Sarah - Problem Solving
"Users struggle with missed deadlines! I'll use Impact vs Effort to prioritize. High impact + Low effort = Do first!"

The Solution: Smart Prioritization

Sarah uses frameworks to make evidence-based decisions, not emotional ones.

Sarah - Success!
"We shipped the MVP! Users love it, and retention improved by 40%. Now let's measure and iterate!"

The Outcome: Measurable Success

Sarah focuses on outcomes (behavior change) not just outputs (features shipped).
